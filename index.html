About

Independent artist and songwriter Zane Karter navigates the complexities of the human experience through a lens of dark R&B and soul. His music is a reflection of a semi-chaotic world, blending somber melodies with atmospheric production to create something deeply resonant. Honest, moody, and ethereal - this is the sound of the dark side of soul.
